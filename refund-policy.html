Refund Policy

Last Updated: October 17, 2025

1. Our 100% Satisfaction Guarantee

At Prime Cleaning NC, we stand by the quality of our service. If you are not completely satisfied with a cleaning service for any reason, please contact us within **24 hours** of the service completion date. We do not offer direct monetary refunds as a first measure. Instead, we offer a **re-clean guarantee**.

We will return to your property within **2 business days** to re-clean any area(s) you are unsatisfied with, at no extra cost to you.

2. Eligibility for Re-Clean or Refund

To be eligible for a complimentary re-clean, you must notify us within the **24-hour window** via email or phone, providing specific details and photos (if possible) of the areas of concern.

Conditions Where Re-Clean May Not Apply:

  • If the customer or another party cleans or attempts to fix the areas before we return.
  • For issues unrelated to the quality of our cleaning (e.g., normal wear and tear, pre-existing damage, or items not covered in the original quote).
  • If the client was present during the cleaning and did not point out the issue before the team left the premises.

3. Monetary Refunds

Monetary refunds are granted only in the event that a re-clean is not possible, or if the re-clean service still fails to meet reasonable quality standards (determined by Prime Cleaning NC management).

If a monetary refund is approved, it will be processed and credited back to your original method of payment within **7-10 business days**. The refund amount may be prorated based on the specific areas of concern.

4. Cancellation Policy

We require a minimum of **48 hours notice** for service cancellations or rescheduling. Cancellations made with less than 48 hours notice may result in a cancellation fee of $50 or 50% of the service cost.
